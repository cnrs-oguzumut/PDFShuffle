Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

PDFGenie+ is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

This app collects no personal information. All PDF operations happen entirely on your device. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

This app does not require internet access and makes no network connections. It works completely offline.
